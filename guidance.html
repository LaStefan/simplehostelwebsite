Location

Because of our location in the center everything is within easy reach. The Parktheather and the Van Abbe museum are within walking distance and the cozy Stratumseind, a street full of bars and eateries is an extension of our street (the Stratumsedijk). The modern center of Eindhoven with its many shops is within 5 minutes and the Philips stadium which is 10 minutes walking distance. in short, the 'bustling' center of Eindhoven has something for everyone.
